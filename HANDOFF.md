## 1. What Has Been Decided — Do Not Re-Litigate

The following decisions are final. A fresh agent must not question or re-open these without explicit instruction from the researcher.

### Dataset Decision: GSE135251 (FINAL)
- **Platform:** Bulk RNA-seq (Illumina), NOT microarray, NOT scRNA-seq
- **Source:** Govaere et al., *Science Translational Medicine* 2020, DOI: [10.1126/scitranslmed.aba4448](https://doi.org/10.1126/scitranslmed.aba4448)
- **n:** ~206 liver biopsies + ~10 controls = 216 total
- **Why chosen:** Largest single NAFLD bulk RNA-seq cohort on GEO; full disease spectrum in one dataset; no batch correction required; field's reference dataset; reused by at least 4 subsequent published papers
- **Why NOT microarray:** Heterogeneous platforms caused batch effects that ComBat could not fully resolve, leading to overfitting and poor generalization in the original multi-dataset pipeline
- **Why NOT scRNA-seq:** NAFLD staging is a tissue-level biopsy diagnosis — scRNA-seq requires pseudo-bulking back to sample level, defeating its purpose; label sparsity (few patients) would be severe
